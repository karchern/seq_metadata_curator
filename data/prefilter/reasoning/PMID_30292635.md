## Criterion 2 — Stool metagenomic (16S or WGS)
- Substrate: mixed (human colon TISSUE for qPCR; mouse FECAL for 16S)
- Technology: 16S (mouse only)
- Status: FAIL
- Quote: "V1-3 regions of Eubacterial 16S rDNA was amplified using primers … the microbial ecology of each sample on the MiSeq" — (Methods 2.11). And in Fig. 1 legend: "Pten mRNA levels were evaluated by qPCR with matched normal tissues (NT) and colon cancer tissues (CT) obtained from colon cancer (CC) patients (11 sets)." Human samples are tissue, not stool; only the mouse cohort has fecal 16S.
- Reasoning: Humans contributed only tumor and adjacent normal tissue biopsies for qPCR — no human stool sequencing. The 16S is on Pten-deficient mice's feces.

## Criterion 3 — Case/control cross-sectional
- Design: mixed (tumor-vs-normal on human tissue for qPCR; genetic mouse KO vs WT for microbiome)
- Status: FAIL
- Quote: "colorectal cancer tissues and adjacent (~10 cm away from the lesion) normal colonic mucosa tissues at two independent medical centers in South Korea" and "Pten floxed (PtenloxP/loxP), Villin promoter-driven Cre expression mouse (VilCre/+), and Apcmin/+ mice" — (Methods 2.1, 2.2)
- Reasoning: Human comparison is tumor vs adjacent-normal tissue (not stool). Mouse comparison is genotype (Pten KO vs WT / Apcmin), not disease case/control.

## Criterion 4 — Illumina platform

- Status: PASS

- Reasoning: Illumina MiSeq confirmed — but only for mouse fecal 16S.

## Criterion 5 — Human subjects
- Species: mixed-human-and-mouse
- Status: FAIL (for the sequenced substrate)
- Quote: "PtenΔIEC/ΔIEC mice were crossed with Apcmin/+ mice to generate PtenΔIEC/ΔIEC; Apcmin/+ mice." and "matched normal tissues (NT) and colon cancer tissues (CT) obtained from colon cancer (CC) patients"
- Reasoning: Humans contributed only tissue for qPCR; the sequenced (fecal 16S) subjects are mice. The human sub-cohort has no sequencing.

## Mixed-content notes
- Human sub-cohort (11 CC patients + 8 healthy controls): tumor tissue and adjacent-normal colonic mucosa; used only for qPCR of Pten mRNA. No 16S or metagenomics on humans.
- Mouse sub-cohort (PtenΔIEC/ΔIEC vs Pten+/+): fecal 16S on Illumina MiSeq.
- No qualifying subset — the sequenced stool cohort is entirely murine.

## Final call
OUT-OF-SCOPE with HIGH confidence. Human component of the study is host-tissue qPCR only; the fecal 16S microbiome data is 100% from Pten-conditional-KO mice. No human stool 16S or shotgun WGS data exists in this paper.
