## Criterion 1 — Primary data
- Status: FAIL
- Quote: "The 16S rRNA gene sequencing data were previously published ( 8 ). Raw sequences were deposited in the NCBI Sequence Read Archive under project accession number PRJNA284355, and processed data files are available in the work of Burns et al. ( 8 )." — (Methods, 16S rRNA sequencing)

- Reasoning: Paired within-subject tumor vs adjacent normal — not a case/control cross-sectional across subjects with a healthy control group. Also this is a CRC-only cohort (all subjects have CRC).
