## Criterion 1 — Primary data
- Status: FAIL
- Quote: "We collected six independent CRC datasets, considering in total 353 patients and their matched normal mucosal samples (when available)' 16S rRNA amplicon sequencing (16S) data (Table 1)... 16S microbial profiles were obtained either by re-analysing the raw data, or by downloading the processed Amplicon sequence variant (ASV) tables" — (Methods, Data Collection)
- Reasoning: All six datasets (Kostic, Hale, Gao, Zeller, Burns, Purcell) are downloaded from prior publications; no new sequencing. Under v2 rulings, re-analysis-only meta-analyses FAIL c1.

## Criterion 2 — Stool metagenomic (16S or WGS)
- Substrate: mucosal tissue (tumor + matched normal mucosa) — NOT stool
- Technology: 16S
- Status: FAIL
- Quote: "Comparing the microbial community compositions between cancerous and adjacent noncancerous tissues" — (Abstract)
- Reasoning: Every included cohort provides TISSUE MUCOSA samples (tumor + matched normal), not stool. Note: the Zeller dataset actually has both fecal + tissue, but this paper explicitly uses only the "48 tumor-normal mucosal pairs" from Zeller — the fecal subset is excluded. All 353 tumor samples analyzed here are tissue mucosa. FAIL substrate.

## Criterion 3 — Case/control cross-sectional
- Design: paired tumor:matched-normal within-subject (in source cohorts)
- Status: FAIL
- Quote: "tumor-normal pairs were subjected to 16S sequencing" — (Methods)
- Reasoning: Within-subject paired tumor vs adjacent normal — not case/control cross-sectional across subjects.

## Criterion 4 — Illumina platform
- Platform(s) named: mixed — Kostic on 454 pyrosequencing (SRP000383), five others on Illumina MiSeq
- Status: PARTIAL
- Quote: "Beside the Kostic dataset, which was based on 454 pyrosequencing, the remaining datasets were all generated by the Illumina MiSeq paired-end platform (Table 1)" — (Methods)
- Reasoning: 5 of 6 datasets are Illumina MiSeq; Kostic is 454 (excluded).

## Criterion 5 — Human subjects
- Species: human
- Status: PASS
- Quote: "353 patients and their matched normal mucosal samples" — (Methods)
- Reasoning: Human patient tissues (inherited).

## Mixed-content notes
- Six source datasets: Kostic (SRP000383, 454, excluded platform), Hale (PRJNA445346), Gao (PRJNA383606), Zeller (PRJEB6070, mucosal-pair subset only), Burns (PRJNA284355), Purcell (PRJNA404030). All are tissue mucosa (tumor + adjacent normal), not stool.
- No new sequencing.

## Final call
OUT-OF-SCOPE with HIGH confidence. Three independent hard failures under v2 rulings: (i) FAIL c1 (re-analysis-only of 6 published cohorts, no new sequencing); (ii) FAIL c2 (substrate is tissue mucosa, not stool); (iii) FAIL c3 (paired within-subject tumor:normal design, not case/control cross-sectional). Any one alone would disqualify.
