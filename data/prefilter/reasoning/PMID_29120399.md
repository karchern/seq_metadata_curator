## Mixed-content notes
- Three technologies used in parallel: HITChip microarray for stool 16S (excluded — not sequencing), 454 pyrosequencing for tissue 16S (excluded — 454 platform), and MiSeq Illumina shotgun metagenomics for both stool and tissue (qualifying subset).
- Qualifying sub-cohort: **20 stool samples (10 adenoma + 10 healthy) shotgun-sequenced on Illumina MiSeq**. Cross-sectional case/control on colorectal adenoma.
- The 20 tissue samples (10 cancer + 10 matched-normal) shotgun-sequenced on MiSeq form a plausible cancer-vs-matched-normal comparison, but substrate is tissue (biopsy), so out for our corpus.
- Validation cohorts (US: 90 normal + 24 polyps + 74 adenomas; HK: 85 normal + 69 adenoma + 78 advanced adenoma + 88 cancer) were assayed by qPCR only — no sequencing produced, so not in corpus.
