## Mixed-content notes
- Human sub-cohort: 5 HD + 5 CRC donors, fecal 16S V3-V4 MiSeq — this is the qualifying human portion.
- Mouse sub-cohort: gut-humanized avatar mice colonized with donor feces; MC38 tumors; anti-PD-1 treatment; own 16S — out of scope for CRC cohort.
- Downstream flag: only the 5-vs-5 donor 16S is human CRC case/control.

## Final call
PARTIAL with LOW confidence. The paper is primarily a mouse anti-PD-1 mechanism study using CRC/HD donor FMT into avatar mice, but includes a tiny (N=5 vs 5) human donor 16S CRC-vs-HD comparison. The human sub-cohort formally qualifies substrate/tech/platform/species/design but is severely underpowered. Include with a "tiny-donor-sub-cohort" flag for downstream selection.
