In this cross-sectional study, we analyzed metabolic syndrome occurrence in 165 patients after curative partial colectomy … and 333 age-sex matched controls. Fecal samples from some of those with RH, LAR, and controls were analyzed by next-generation sequencing method." — (Abstract)

## Criterion 3 — Case/control cross-sectional
- Design: cross-sectional-case-control (post-CRC-resection survivors vs healthy controls)
- Status: PARTIAL
- Quote: "165 subjects at outpatient clinics successively who had underwent curative surgery including right hemicolectomy (RH) or low anterior resection (LAR) for primary nondisseminated CRC in Taipei Veterans General Hospital between 2006–2010 were identified and enrolled in our study group … 1:2 age-sex, time of follow-up matched subjects without surgery for GI tract were also enrolled as the control group" — (Methods, Study population)
- Reasoning: Case group is post-curative-colectomy CRC survivors (~8.5 years post-op), not active CRC — comparison is essentially "post-CRC + altered anatomy" vs healthy. The disease group's microbiota reflects surgery-induced anatomical change, not CRC disease. Not a canonical case/control for CRC-microbiome, but formally cross-sectional with CRC-history cases vs controls. PARTIAL.

## Mixed-content notes
- Sequenced sub-cohort is 10 RH + 10 LAR + 20 controls (n=40 total fecal 16S), a subset of the full 165+333 metabolic-syndrome cohort.
- Case group is post-colectomy CRC survivors, not treatment-naive active CRC. Downstream analysis flag: "post-CRC-resection" not naive CRC.
- INSDC accession: PRJEB32249 (ENA).

## Final call
PARTIAL with MEDIUM confidence. Substrate, technology, platform, and species all pass; design is a cross-sectional cohort comparing long-term CRC survivors (after right-hemi or LAR resection) to age/sex-matched controls without prior GI surgery. Because the "case" arm is post-surgical rather than treatment-naive CRC, the microbiota signal is confounded with surgical-anatomy change rather than pure CRC disease. Include with a "post-CRC-resection-vs-HC" flag for downstream selection.
