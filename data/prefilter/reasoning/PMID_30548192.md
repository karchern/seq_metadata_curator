## Criterion 3 — Case/control cross-sectional
- Design: cross-sectional-case-control (4 groups: colorectal adenoma, CRC pre-op, CRC post-op, healthy controls)
- Status: PASS
- Quote: "16S rRNA gene sequencing was used to evaluate differences in gut microbiota among colorectal adenoma patients, CRC patients, CRC postoperative patients and healthy controls" — (Summary)
- Reasoning: Four-group cross-sectional comparison with healthy controls, colorectal adenoma, CRC, and postoperative CRC. Disease scope is squarely CRC + adenoma. Follow-up samples are collected but the primary analysis is cross-sectional between groups (postoperative snapshot is analyzed at one timepoint per subject).

## Mixed-content notes
- Four groups: colorectal adenoma, CRC pre-op, CRC post-op (subdivided into NDA = newly-developed adenoma vs CIT = clean intestine), healthy controls. 47 follow-up samples 6-36 months post-surgery.
- The post-op subgroups (NDA vs CIT) form a within-CRC-patient stratification.

## Final call
IN-SCOPE with HIGH confidence. All five criteria pass. Chinese cohort with cross-sectional case/control comparison across colorectal adenoma, CRC pre-op, CRC post-op, and healthy controls; stool 16S V3-V4 on Illumina MiSeq PE300. Registered clinical trial NCT03385213.
