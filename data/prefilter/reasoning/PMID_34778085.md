## Criterion 1 — Primary data
- Status: PASS
- Quote: "We profiled the gene expressions and microbe compositions of colonic mucosa from patients with solid cancers receiving anti-PD-L1 antibody treatment; we then compared the expression profiles associated with irAE colitis with those associated with UC." — (Abstract)
- Reasoning: Authors' own Kindai University cohort with new transcriptome + 16S.

## Criterion 2 — Stool metagenomic (16S or WGS)
- Substrate: mostly colonic biopsy tissue (plus 3 healthy fecal samples)
- Technology: mixed (16S V3-V4 on Illumina MiSeq for inactive/inflamed tissue; 16S multi-region on Ion S5 for active tissue)
- Status: FAIL
- Quote: "biopsy tissue from an inflamed region (active) and a non-inflamed region (inactive) were collected. … For controls, biopsies were taken from uninflamed mucosae from 3 non-IBD patients and feces samples were collected from 3 healthy individuals." — (Methods, Patients and Controls)
- Reasoning: Substrate is intestinal mucosal biopsy tissue for cases (irAE + UC); only 3 healthy individuals contributed fecal samples. The disease-arm substrate is biopsy, not stool. Additionally, mixed 16S platforms include Ion Torrent S5 (non-Illumina).

## Criterion 3 — Case/control cross-sectional
- Design: cross-sectional (irAE colitis vs UC vs healthy controls; disease is not CRC)
- Status: FAIL
- Quote: "Eighteen patients with immune checkpoint inhibitor-induced (irAE) colitis, 9 with ulcerative colitis (UC), and three healthy individuals were included in this study." — (Methods, Patients and Controls)
- Reasoning: Per v2 ruling, disease scope must be CRC / colorectal adenoma / colorectal dysplasia. irAE colitis and UC are inflammatory conditions, not CRC. FAIL c3.

## Criterion 4 — Illumina platform
- Platform(s) named: Illumina MiSeq (V3 300-bp PE) for inactive tissue; Thermo Fisher Ion S5 for active tissue
- Status: PARTIAL
- Quote: "16S rDNA sequencing using the Illumina MiSeq platform was performed on inactive intestinal biopsy tissue samples … 16S rDNA sequencing using the Thermo Fisher Scientific Ion S5 platform was performed on active intestinal biopsy tissue samples." — (Methods, 16S rDNA Sequencing)
- Reasoning: Mixed Illumina + Ion Torrent per v2 ruling — Illumina subset would conditionally qualify on platform alone, but disease and substrate fail independently.

## Criterion 5 — Human subjects
- Species: human
- Status: PASS
- Quote: "18 patients with irAE colitis … 9 with ulcerative colitis (UC), and three healthy individuals" — (Methods)

## Mixed-content notes
- Case cohort (irAE colitis + UC): colonic biopsy tissue on mixed MiSeq + Ion S5.
- Control cohort: 3 healthy individuals with fecal samples + 3 non-IBD biopsy controls.
- No qualifying sub-cohort: disease group has no stool, and disease is not CRC.

## Final call
OUT-OF-SCOPE with HIGH confidence. Study of immune-related-adverse-event colitis vs ulcerative colitis using colonic biopsy tissue transcriptome + 16S (mixed MiSeq + Ion S5). Fails c2 (biopsy, not stool for cases; plus Ion Torrent mix) and c3 (disease is irAE colitis / UC, not CRC).
