## Criterion 1 — Primary data
- Status: FAIL
- Quote: "We analyzed 2154 publicly available gut metagenomic samples from twelve studies. We considered the nine largest metagenomics studies we were aware of and were available as of July 2015 to which we added three additional studies to expand the geographical span" — (Materials and methods)
- Reasoning: This is a re-analysis of 12 previously published public metagenomic datasets to profile Blastocystis. No new sequencing performed; per v2 ruling, re-analysis-only papers FAIL criterion 1.

- Technology: shotgun-WGS
- Status: PASS

- Reasoning: Substrate is gut/stool metagenomes; technology is shotgun WGS. Would pass in isolation.

## Criterion 3 — Case/control cross-sectional
- Design: other:multi-cohort-reanalysis (heterogeneous diseases)
- Status: FAIL
- Quote: "Eight of the considered studies aimed at characterizing the human gut in different health conditions whereas four considered subjects not affected by documented medical conditions... T2D, CD, UC, Liver cirrhosis, Colorectal cancer, Obesity, STEC infection" — (Materials and methods / Table 1)
- Reasoning: Pooled multi-disease meta-analysis of 12 datasets across T2D, CD/UC, cirrhosis, obesity, CRC (Zeller et al.), STEC, healthy. Not a single CRC case/control design; per v2 disease scope, this fails c3 (case group is not CRC-specific).

- Status: NOT-STATED
- Quote: "The selected raw metagenomes were processed with FastqMcf... Human DNA and Illumina spike-in DNA (Bacteriophage phiX174) were then removed" — (Materials and methods)

## Criterion 5 — Human subjects
- Species: human
- Status: PASS
- Quote: "1689 subjects from 18 different countries and 4 continents (Europe, Africa, Asia and North/South America) were studied" — (Introduction)

## Mixed-content notes
Re-analysis of 12 public gut metagenomes: Candela, HMP, Karlsson, Le Chatelier, Liu, Loman, MetaHIT, Nielsen, Obregon-Tito, Qin, T2D (Qin 2012), Zeller. Zeller 2014 is the CRC-relevant subset; the others are out of scope diseases (T2D, cirrhosis, IBD, obesity, STEC). No new samples added.

## Final call
OUT-OF-SCOPE with HIGH confidence. Beghini et al. 2017 is a re-analysis-only paper pooling 12 published gut metagenomic cohorts to characterize Blastocystis prevalence. Per v2 ruling, re-analysis-only papers fail criterion 1 (no new primary sequencing). Also spans many non-CRC diseases so c3 does not apply cleanly.
