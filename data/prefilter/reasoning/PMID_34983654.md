## Criterion 3 — Case/control cross-sectional
- Design: single-arm longitudinal prospective observational (pre-op → intraop → 3-day post-op) with antimicrobial-prophylaxis stratification
- Status: FAIL
- Quote: "single-centre prospective observational cohort study to evaluate the changes in the gut microbiota, i.e., taxon distribution, before and after elective oncologic colon surgery in adult patients with different antimicrobial prophylaxis regimens (standard prophylaxis with cefuroxime/metronidazole versus carbapenems for extended-spectrum beta-lactamase-producing Enterobacterales [ESBL-E] carriers)" — (Methods)
- Reasoning: Longitudinal same-subject pre/intra/post-surgical sampling, all subjects are CRC patients — NO healthy control group. Primary analysis is surgery-induced change + drug-arm comparison, not case/control cross-sectional CRC-vs-healthy. Under v2, this is not a case/control cross-sectional design.

## Final call

Illumina MiSeq platform passes but is irrelevant.
