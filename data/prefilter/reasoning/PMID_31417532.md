## Mixed-content notes
- Very small cohort (N=18 total). Three sub-groups: L-CRC (5), L-GC (5, gynecological cancer only — OUT of CRC scope), healthy (8).
- Only the L-CRC (5) vs healthy (8) subset qualifies as CRC vs control. The L-GC vs healthy comparison is out of scope.
- All CRC-side cases are Lynch syndrome carriers (hereditary CRC) rather than sporadic CRC — worth flagging for cohort characterization.
- All patients are postoperative from prior cancer surgery. This is a case-group characteristic, not a longitudinal design; cross-sectional case-control shape preserved.

## Final call
PARTIAL with HIGH confidence. Only the 5 L-CRC (LS carriers with prior CRC or dysplastic polyps) vs 8 healthy Italian female controls form an in-scope stool 16S MiSeq case/control cross-sectional comparison. The L-GC (5, gynecological cancer only) subgroup is out of CRC scope. Pilot sample size is very small (5+8=13 qualifying samples).
