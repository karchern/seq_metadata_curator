## Mixed-content notes
Four groups: C (n=33 healthy) + I (n=17 CRC pre-tx) + D (n=14 chemo-treated CRC) + IO (n=5 surgery-treated CRC). The C vs I comparison is the clean case/control cross-section; D and IO are post-treatment strata within CRC. All samples appear to be single-timepoint per subject (not longitudinal follow-up of same subjects). The 33+17=50 baseline case/control samples map cleanly to scope; the 19 treated samples are additional strata for downstream analysis.

## Final call
PARTIAL with HIGH confidence. Clean C-vs-I case/control on stool 16S / Illumina / human / CRC easily passes; but the paper also includes post-treatment CRC arms (chemo, surgery) which are treatment-vs-untreated strata within CRC (not clean case/control). Downstream mapper should pull the healthy + CRC-baseline arms cleanly; treated arms can serve as additional CRC-only stratified subsets.
