                                             Colorectal cancer (CRC) is the third most common cancer worldwide. Its incidence is still
                                             increasing, and the mortality rate is high. New therapeutic and prognostic strategies are
                                             urgently needed. It became increasingly recognized that the gut microbiota composition
                                             differs significantly between healthy people and CRC patients. Thus, identifying the
                                             difference between gut microbiota of the healthy people and CRC patients is fundamental
                                             to understand these microbes’ functional roles in the development of CRC. We studied
