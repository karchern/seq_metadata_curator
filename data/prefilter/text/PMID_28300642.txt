                                                                                             2.8. Phylogenetic analysis

                                                                                                 The 16S rRNA gene sequences obtained from the biopsy speci-
                                                                                             mens were aligned and compared with previously published Bra-
                                                                                             chyspira spp. sequences available from GenBank using the software
                                                                                             HUSAR 4.1. A neighbor-joining phylogenetic tree was constructed
                                                                                             on the basis of a distance matrix corrected by the two-parameter
                                                                                             model of Tamura and Nei [47,48]. The type strains of B. aalborgi,
                                                                                             B. pilosicoli, B. innocens, B. murdochii, B. intermedia,
                                                                                             B. hyodysenteriae, B. alvinipulli, and ”B. canis” as well as six other
                                                                                             B. aalborgi, B. pilosicoli, “B. ibaraki,” “B. hominis,” “B. corvi,” B. pulli,
                                                                                             B. hampsonii and “B. suanatina” strains, were included. The 16S
                                                                                             rRNA gene sequence of a distantly related species of spirochetes,
                                                                                             Borrelia burgdorferi B31T (accession number U03396), was included
                                                                                             as outgroup. The alignment was trimmed to a uniform length of 153
                                                                                             nucleotides. Bootstrapping values were calculated (1000 replicates)
                                                                                             to assess the statistical reliability of the generated dendrogram and
                                                                                             percentage values were placed at the major nodes. Bootstrapping,
                                                                                             distance matrix calculation, neighbor joining and consensus tree
                                                                                             calculation were done with MEGA 5.1. software [49,50].
