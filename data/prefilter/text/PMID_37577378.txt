                                                   Conclusion: Our data indicate that different external and internal factors drive
                                                   comparable proﬁles of low diversity dysbiosis. While diarrheal-related low
                                                   diversity dysbiosis may be a consequence of systemic cancer therapy, a similar
                                                   phenotype is observed in cases of moderate to severe IBD, and in both cases,
                                                   dysbiosis is exacerbated by incidence of CDI.
