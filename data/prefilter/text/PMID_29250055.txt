                                             Oral leukoplakia presents as a white patch on the oral mucosa and is recognized
                                             as having significant malignant potential. Although colonization of these patches with
                                             Candida albicans is common, little is known about the bacterial microbiota of these
                                             patches. In the current study we analyzed the microbiome of oral leukoplakia in 36
                                             patients compared to healthy mucosal tissue from the same patients and healthy
                                             control subjects to determine if specific microbial enrichments could be identified early
